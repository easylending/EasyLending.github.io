Easy Capital Group provides small business loan alternatives to small business owners who own existing businesses (must have business bank account setup + at least 6 months in business). We fund all types of business including those who do not qualify for traditional bank loans due to bad credit, limited credit history, high risk designation or any other reason. We do not require business plans to approve funding, and we need no personal guarantee of collateral from business owners seeking our merchant cash advances.
